Who we are

FIBACT professional team is comprised of former Big Four CPA's, Enrolled Agents, MBA’s & Chartered Accountants with extensive knowledge and experience in the areas of Finance, Accounting, Advisory and Taxation.The team has global exposure working in India, US and UK providing services to Individuals, Corporations and Financial Services clients.

We also specialize in US Expat Tax Services - Non-U.S. residents who may need to file taxes in the United States.
We serve international business clients requiring financial reporting in accordance to U.S. Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) and International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS).
We promptly and efficiently utilize internet technology to assist our clients - across all time zones.

Why choose us

FIBACT is formed with the objective to provide Bookkeeping, Accounting and Tax Compliance Services to Accounting Firms, Small and Medium Enterprises in US & India. We offer a broad range of services to Accounting firms by partnering with them as their extended team and provide them the resources required to serve their clients. We enable Start-ups, Small & Medium Enterprises to focus on their core area of expertise, while we manage their compliance requirement, book keeping, financial reporting and tax return preparations.
